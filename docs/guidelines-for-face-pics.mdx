# Guidelines for Face Pictures

![guidelines](/assets/main/guideline.png)

### Tips for Taking Face Pictures

- Use a recent photo (within 3 months).
- Ensure proper lighting and a white background.
- Avoid shadows, reflections, or smiles.
- Take photos with clear visibility of eyebrows and ears.
- Look straight ahead with a level gaze.
- Avoid covering your eyebrows, eyes, nose, and mouth as much as possible.
