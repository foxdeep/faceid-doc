### Overview

The **Time Cards** page let users view the time card records of all employees. The page displays the following information:

- **Profile Photo**: The employee's profile photo.
- **Member**: The employee's member name and UID.
- **Timestamp of ID Card**: The time the employee swiped their ID card.
- **Timestamp of Face Recognition**: The time the employee was recognized by the face recognition system.
- **Capture face**: The face image captured by the face recognition system.
- **Finished status**: The status of the time card record.
- **Buddy**: The buddy who helped the employee check in or out.

---

### Search and Filter

![time-card-filter](/assets/attendance/time-card-filter.png)

- **Search**: Enter the employee's UID in the search bar to find the corresponding time card record.
- **Filter**: Filter the time card records by **Time Range**, **Buddy**, **Type**, **Identity**, and **Finished Status**.

---

### Export

- Click the **Export** button to export the time card records to a CSV file. Users can choose to export summary or detailed records.
