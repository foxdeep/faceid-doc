### Overview

The **Meeting** page allows administrators to manage meeting records, including creating, editing, and deleting meetings.

---

### Steps to Create a Meeting

1. Click the **red “+” button** in the bottom-right corner.
2. Enter the meeting details, including the **Name**, **Location** (optional), **Start Time**, **End Time**, **Clock in before start** and **Attendees**.

   ![meeting](/assets/attendance/meeting.png)

3. Click the **Attendees** button to select the attendees from the list of members; click the **Apply** button to add the selected attendees to the meeting.

   ![attendees](/assets/attendance/meeting-select-attendee.png)

4. Click the **Save** button to create the meeting.
