### Overview

The Dashboard is your go-to center for tracking ESD compliance, offering:

- **Total active teams** – Displays the number of teams currently enrolled in ESD tracking.
- **Expired Buddy Licenses** – Indicates the number of buddy licenses that need renewal for ESD compliance.
- **Binding Buddies** – Provides a list of buddies connected to specific devices, along with their online or offline status.
- **Daily Clock in Overview** – Gives a visual overview of completed and failed ESD tests throughout the day.
- **Latest Clock in Record** – Displays the most recent clock-in and clock-out timestamps, along with the associated UIDs and buddy information.

With this information, you can easily monitor ESD compliance trends, identify any failed tests, and ensure adherence to ESD safety policies.
